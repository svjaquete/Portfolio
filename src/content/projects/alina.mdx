<Problem
  label="The problem?"
  heading="Large industrial users had no precise way to monitor or control their water consumption."
>
  Construction companies and manufacturers consume significant amounts of water but relied on manual readings and end-of-month invoices to understand usage — making leak detection, optimization, and accurate reporting nearly impossible.
</Problem>

<SectionTitle title="Alignment" />

<DescRow label="Stakeholder workshops">
  Workshops to define project objectives and scope, establishing shared understanding across the client and design team before research began.
</DescRow>

<SectionTitle title="Research" />

<DescRow label="Interviews">
  Sessions with water monitoring professionals across two industries: **construction** and **skincare manufacturing** — two very different consumption patterns and reporting needs.
</DescRow>

<DescRow label="Market Research">
  Competitive analysis of existing industrial water monitoring solutions to identify gaps and benchmark best practices.
</DescRow>
