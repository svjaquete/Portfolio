<Problem
  label="The problem?"
  heading="Loan follow-up operations were scattered across teams with no unified system."
>
  After a health loan was approved, managing it — tracking customers, updating status, handling documents, calculating payments — was done manually across disconnected systems, causing delays and errors for banks, medical centers, and doctors alike.
</Problem>

<SectionTitle title="Research & Strategy" />

<DescRow label="Stakeholder Interviews">
  Qualitative sessions with financial institution staff and health center administrators to map pain points and understand the current operational reality.
</DescRow>

<DescRow label="Alignment">
  Workshop to align all teams — banks, medical centers, doctors — on shared priorities and goals before design began.
</DescRow>

<DescRow label="Blueprint">
  A detailed service blueprint mapping every touchpoint and interaction across all stakeholders in the service journey.
</DescRow>

<SectionTitle title="Define" />

<DescRow label="Architecture">

</DescRow>

  - Customer profile tracking and loan lifecycle management.
